General & About Ekyamm

Learn the basics about who we are and what we do

Ekyamm is a mental wellness platform that empowers fertility treatment plans by seamlessly integrating emotional well-being support into the patient's journey. We partner with clinics to provide guided exercises, mood tracking, and daily check-ins through our mobile app — at zero cost to the clinic.

Ekyamm is developed by Radicle Minds India Private Limited, based in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. Our team includes mental health professionals, fertility specialists, and technology experts dedicated to improving patient outcomes through emotional care.

Ekyamm works through a simple three-step process:

  • Doctor Prescribes: Your fertility specialist recommends Ekyamm as part of your treatment plan.
  • Patient Downloads: You download the Ekyamm app and get access to personalised emotional support tools.
  • Ongoing Support: The app provides guided exercises, mood tracking, and daily check-ins throughout your fertility journey.

Currently, Ekyamm primarily partners with fertility clinics across India. We are actively exploring expansion to other regions. Please contact us for the latest availability.

Ekyamm has been recognised as the "Mental Health Start-Up of the Year" and is a "Psy Shark Tank Winner." These accolades reflect our commitment to evidence-based emotional care in the fertility space.

Services & How It Works

Understand what Ekyamm offers patients and couples

Your fertility doctor will prescribe Ekyamm as part of your treatment plan. Once prescribed, simply download the Ekyamm app from the App Store or Google Play, create your account, and begin your personalised emotional wellness program.

The Ekyamm app provides a comprehensive emotional wellness toolkit including:

  • Guided relaxation and mindfulness exercises
  • Daily mood tracking and emotional check-ins
  • Personalised coping strategies for fertility-related stress
  • Progress reports shared with your care team (with your consent)

Ekyamm is designed to be prescribed by your fertility doctor as part of your treatment plan. This ensures the emotional support you receive is coordinated with your medical care. If your clinic partners with Ekyamm, your doctor can prescribe it directly.

Yes, fertility journeys affect both partners. Ekyamm is designed to support couples through the process together. Please check with your clinic about partner access and any additional features available for couples.

The program duration is tailored to your treatment journey and can continue throughout your fertility treatment. Your care team will guide the recommended duration based on your individual needs.

Clinical & Treatment

Evidence-based information about our approach to care

Privacy & Security

How we protect your data and ensure compliance
